Coding for Catapults

An eager-to-learn group of rising 11th and 12th graders from the nextech Catapult program completed a 3 week coding camp at Kenzie Academy. During this intensive course they learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and applied them to create several interactive apps, games, and experiences on their personal portfolio websites.
